Documents Required


1) Proof of Income

Steps to apply


1. The application process for this scheme is online.
2. Go to the Digital Gujarat Portal and register on it.
3. Select this scheme on the portal and the online application form will appear.
4. You can also download the hard copy of the form from there, fill it up, attach the documents and submit it to the OBC welfare department.
5. Fill all the rows and columns of the online form and upload all the documents.
6. Click on 'Submit'.
7. The selected applications will be forwarded to your school, and you will receive the benefit amount directly on your bank account.
