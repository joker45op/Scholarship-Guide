National Agriculture Insurance Scheme

This scheme provides financial assistance to farmers in the event of crop loss due to natural calamities such as flood, hailstorms,etc.

Price Support Scheme

This scheme provides financial assistance to farmers when the market price of their produce falls below the minimum support price declared by the government.

AGR-2 Scheme

It provides financial assistance to farmers for purchasing agricultural equipment and implements, thereby reducing their operational costs and improving farm productivity.
